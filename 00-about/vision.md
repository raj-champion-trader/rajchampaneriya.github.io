## Guiding Principles

1. **Quality over Quantity** - One strong piece beats ten shallow posts
2. **Clarity over Cleverness** - Show how you think, not how smart you are
3. **Practical over Theoretical** - Real experience, not textbook knowledge
4. **Long-term over Short-term** - Building for years, not months
5. **Ownership over Platform** - Own your content, control your brand
