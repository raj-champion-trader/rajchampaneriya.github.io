# About

This folder contains your identity layer - the foundation of your personal brand.

## Files to Create

### about-me.md
Your professional bio and background.
- 2-3 line positioning statement
- 5 bullet strengths
- "What I write about" (3 themes max)

### vision.md
Your professional vision and goals.
- Why you create content
- Long-term objectives
- Value you bring to readers

### content-philosophy.md
Your approach to content creation.
- Writing principles
- Topics you care about
- How you think about teaching/sharing

---

**Status**: Foundation for your personal manifesto
**Audience**: Recruiters, reviewers, potential clients
