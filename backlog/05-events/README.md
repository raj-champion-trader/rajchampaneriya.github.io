# Events - Credibility Ledger

Document all speaking engagements, talks, workshops, and professional events.

## Why This Matters

This folder is your **trust builder** and **credibility proof**:
- Shows leadership visibility
- Critical for IBM internal reviews
- Valuable for future consulting work
- Demonstrates influence and reach

## Event Categories

### conferences/
Major industry conferences where you spoke, attended, or participated.

### meetups/
Local or community meetups, user groups, technical presentations.

### internal-talks/
Company-internal presentations, lunch & learns, team training sessions.

### workshops/
Hands-on workshops, training sessions you led or facilitated.

## Event Documentation Format

Each event gets its own markdown file: `YYYY-MM-DD-event-name.md`

### Template Structure

```markdown
# [Event Name]

**Date**: [Date]  
**Location**: [City/Online]  
**Your Role**: [Speaker / Panelist / Attendee / Organizer]

## Topic/Title
[Your presentation title or participation topic]

## Description
[Brief description of the event and your contribution]

## Key Takeaways
- [Learning or insight 1]
- [Learning or insight 2]
- [Learning or insight 3]

## Materials
- Slides: [Link or file]
- Recording: [Link if available]
- Photos: [Link to photos folder]

## Reflection
[What you learned, how it went, what you'd do differently]

## Impact
[Who was there, connections made, follow-ups]
```

## What to Include

✅ **Include**:
- Conference talks (even short ones)
- Internal IBM presentations
- Meetup presentations
- Panel discussions
- Workshop facilitation
- Podcast appearances

❌ **Don't Worry About**:
- Every meeting you attend
- One-on-one conversations
- Regular team syncs

---

**Purpose**: Build visible track record of influence and expertise  
**Maintenance**: Update immediately after each event  
**Review**: Quarterly review for LinkedIn/resume updates
