# Backlog - Brain Dump

Your pressure-free idea collection and content pipeline.

## Purpose

This is where you:
- Dump thoughts without commitment
- Track potential topics
- Plan future content
- Review what resonates

## Files

### ideas.md
Raw idea collection. No filtering, no pressure.

Format: Simple bullet list with context.

### content-pipeline.md
Organized view of upcoming content.

Tracks:
- Next Week
- Next Month
- Next Quarter
- Someday/Maybe

### future-topics.md
Curated list of topics you want to explore deeply.

Categories:
- Architecture patterns to document
- Technologies to learn and share
- Lessons to reflect on
- Books to review

## Usage Pattern

### Weekly Review (5 minutes)
- Add any new ideas that came up
- Move one idea to content-pipeline.md
- Archive completed topics

### Monthly Planning (15 minutes)
- Review backlog
- Identify 4-5 topics for next month
- Align with content rotation schedule

## Rules

1. **No judging ideas** - Write it down first, filter later
2. **No deadlines** - These are possibilities, not commitments
3. **No guilt** - Old ideas can stay forever or be deleted
4. **Yes to duplicates** - If you thought of it twice, it matters

## Idea Capture Format

```markdown
## Topic/Idea Title

**Type**: [Blog/Video/Kata/Exercise]  
**Why interesting**: [Quick note]  
**Potential angle**: [How you'd approach it]  
**Date added**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
```

Simple enough to add in 30 seconds.
